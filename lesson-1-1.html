🎯 Learning Objective

I can solve one-step equations by using inverse operations and checking whether my answer makes the equation true.

📚 Learn the Strategy

Big idea: An equation is balanced. Whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other side.

x + 5 = 12
1. Identify what is happening to the variable: 5 is being added.
2. Use the inverse operation: subtract 5 from both sides.
3. The solution is x = 7 because 7 + 5 = 12.
